Frequently asked questions



1.What is Zayla?

Zayla is a leading e-commerce platform offering a wide range of products including fashion, electronics, home essentials, and more — all at affordable prices and with convenient doorstep delivery.


2. How can I place an order on Zayla?

You can easily browse products on our website, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout using secure payment methods like UPI, credit/debit cards, or cash on delivery..


3.Does Zayla offer free shipping?

Yes, we offer free shipping on orders above ₹499. For orders below this amount, a small shipping fee may apply depending on your location.


4.What is Zayla’s return and refund policy?

.We offer a 7-day return policy for most products. If you're not satisfied, you can request a return or exchange. Refunds are processed within 5–7 working days after product inspection.


5.. How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking ID via email and SMS. You can also log in to your Zayla account and check the order status under "My Orders".
